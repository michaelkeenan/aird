Thank you for reading through this interactive guide on the potential risks from advanced AI systems. We hope that it has been an engaging experience, and that it felt like a productive exploration of part of the discussion about the development and deployment of highly capable AI.

This series was originally based on arguments and counterarguments discussed during a set of interviews Vael Gates had with 97 AI researchers, and we’re eager to add your insights and perspectives. Your beliefs, in terms of agreements and disagreements, have been added to the plots below describing what readers of this site think about potential risks from AI (INCLUDE PLOTS).
