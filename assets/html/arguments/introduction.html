​​Welcome to this interactive guide on the potential risks from advanced AI systems! We're glad you're here and interested in exploring this aspect of the discussion on the development and deployment of highly capable AI systems.
As you go through this guide, you'll encounter a range of arguments and counterarguments related to the potential risks of advanced AI. At the bottom of each page, you'll have the opportunity to select between several answers or counterarguments. We encourage you to think critically and engage with the material presented here, and welcome your disagreement or agreement with various perspectives.
The arguments and counterarguments presented in this guide are based on interviews with approximately 100 AI researchers, and reflect the most common perspectives within that population. (You’ll see some quotes embedded in this guide; those quotes are from interviewees.) If your own views are not represented here, we welcome your feedback and encourage you to get in touch with us.
